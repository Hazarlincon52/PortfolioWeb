About Me

  • Currently, I have graduated from college with a bachelor's degree in Informatics Engineering at Binus, Alam Sutra University. My hobby while studying and working was designing SFX and Foley sounds for animation and especially video games. I discovered this hobby when I was involved in learning activities, teaching, activism, and management of BGDC (Binus Game Development Club), and so on until now, sound design, audio programming, and game development are the goals and passions that I most want to develop in the world of work and can provide the best in the games created or other intermediary media.
